Ankinox offers indie studios and independent creators a professional platform to showcase their work to a broad audience.

What we offer studios:

  • Reach & Community: We bundle our community to put your films in the spotlight.
  • Transparent Partnership: Fair annual contracts with maximum flexibility.
  • Fair Revenue Share: Percentage share of advertising revenue.
  • Full-Service: We handle hosting, integration, and community work.

How does the collaboration work?

We place great value on planning reliability for all parties involved. If a collaboration should ever end, we guarantee a one-month notice period from the date of termination. This gives us the time to inform our community transparently and cleanly remove the content from the program – for a fair and professional parting.
